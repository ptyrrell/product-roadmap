## AE (Sales) Team Equivalents

A good sales manager wants to know three things:
**Are they active? Are they moving deals forward? Is anything going stale?**

| SDR Metric | AE Equivalent | Why it matters |
|------------|--------------|----------------|
| Outbound calls made | Follow-up / nurture calls made | Proves they're not waiting passively |
| Connects | Live conversations with active deals | Quality engagement, not just voicemails |
| Discovery calls booked | Demos booked | Pipeline input |
| Discovery calls attended | Demos completed | Show rate / deal momentum |
| — | Proposals / quotes sent | Commitment signal from prospect |
| — | Trials activated (logged a job) | Key FieldInsight conversion milestone |
| Wins | Wins / closed-won | Revenue output |

---

## The Two Extras That Matter Most for AEs

### 1. Days Since Last Activity (Deal Age)
This is the single most important AE-specific metric.  
Every active deal should show when it was last touched.  
Anything over **5–7 business days** without activity = stale deal.  
A good sales manager flags these daily.

### 2. Next Action Scheduled
Not just "did something happen" — but "is there a concrete next step booked?"  
A deal with **no next action scheduled** is effectively dead.
