💍 One Ring to Rule Them All — Primary Focus
Email / SMS / VOIP Scheduler — AI Interaction Model
The centrepiece build. Customers book, reschedule, and communicate with FieldInsight entirely through their preferred channel — email thread, SMS reply, or VOIP call. A unified AI layer (Claude primary, Grok swappable) understands intent across all three channels, maps it to job state, and responds naturally. The scheduler drives outbound; customers drive inbound. One conversation per job, regardless of channel.
🔥 Active High 🏗 Product 🎨 Design Claude ⇄ Grok
