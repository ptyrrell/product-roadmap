  • 📦 AIRTEK GCSMSB — BACnet IP SMS alarm module, alerts up to 10 phones, GSM 900/1800MHz, RS232 config. Enterprise BMS grade.

  • 🔌 Daikin — D-BACS BACnet interface (up to 512 indoor units) or DIII-NET/Modbus RS485 adapter. Daikin M2M cloud + AU monitoring app (needs BTSC/i2S adapter).
  • 🔌 Mitsubishi Electric — MICRO M2M SMS Interface (up to 8 units, direct SMS fault codes) or PANEL_RS_SMS (includes fire alarm input). ME-AC-SMS-32 for larger installs.
  • 🔌 Panasonic — HMS Networks MQTT Gateway (ECOi/PACi to AWS/Azure), CZ-CFUNC2 BMS serial adapter, or PAW-AC-MBS-1 Modbus interface.
  • Configuration methods: WiFi app · Bluetooth · QR code scan · RS485 wired · BACnet IP

  • Inbound SMS fault webhook → parse fault code → lookup error library → classify severity

  • Technician briefed with full fault history before attending site
  • Failure trend dashboard — units, fault types, clients, repeat offenders

🐐 Mountain Goat Farm — Gippsland, VIC
Personal project. Hilly treed property in Gippsland, Victoria. GPS plot measured — scoping fencing, stocking plan, breed selection, and revenue model for a small mountain goat operation.
🌿 Personal 🔬 Research
  • 📍 View property on Google Maps →
  • Location: -37.533°S, 147.276°E — Gippsland, Victoria (hilly, treed terrain)
  • GPS plot: 75.29m paddock boundary measured on-site
  • Best breeds for this terrain (researched):
  • 🥇 Kiko — top pick for hilly/mountainous AU terrain. NZ-developed for harsh climates. Parasite-resistant, hardy, excellent mothering. Outlasts Boer 2:1 on survival in humid conditions.
  • 🥈 Boer — fast-growing meat breed, up to 160kg, but lower longevity in humid Gippsland-style climates. Best as a cross with Kiko.
  • 🥉 Saanen — if dairy angle pursued. Gold standard milk producer for cooler Victoria/Tas climates. 4L/day. Suits Gippsland winters well.
  • 🌿 British Alpine — milks through winter, good on free-range hilly systems.
  • Economics (2025 farmgate):
  • Meat: 282–305 cents/kg carcase weight. Export ~$4,725/tonne.
  • Sustainable breakeven target: $4.50–$6.00/kg live weight
  • Export market strong — AU processed record 2.9M goats in 2024-25 (+47% YoY)
  • Setup requirements:
  • Stock-tight fencing + electric backup — goats are escape artists on hilly ground
  • All-weather 3-sided shelter, dry and draft-free with straw bedding
  • Stocking rate: max 8 DSE/hectare to control parasites
  • Goats browse weeds and scrubland — natural vegetation management benefit
  • Next steps: confirm land size, quote fencing contractor, source Kiko stock
