Onboarding Conquest

The fastest path to market domination is frictionless migration. Operators who can't get their data out of simPRO or ServiceM8 in under a week will not switch. This is the ranked build plan — start at P1, unlock the market as you go.

# Component Effort Impact
1 CSV import top-up for Tradify + generic platforms Low Immediate — covers the gap where no API exists. Tradify has 10,000+ ANZ installs. Generic CSV covers any platform FieldInsight doesn't have an extractor for yet.
2 simPRO API extraction microservice Medium Highest value — simPRO is the dominant ANZ platform. A one-click "Extract from simPRO" wizard converts the migration from a blocker into a differentiator. Target: reactive maintenance operators frustrated by simPRO's project-centric workflow.
3 ServiceM8 extraction Low–Medium Second largest ANZ install base — popular with small residential trades (HVAC, plumbing, electrical). Their API is well-documented. Captures the segment graduating from basic tools.
4 Outbound AI enrichment pipeline Medium Multiplier on existing outbound. Enriches imported data with AI-inferred fields — typical job durations, common parts by trade/postcode, pricing benchmarks. Makes FieldInsight smarter from day one of import, not after 6 months of use.
5 Pre-demo migration preview flow Medium Highest conversion impact. Prospect connects their existing system before the demo. They see their own jobs, assets, and customers inside FieldInsight before they've committed. Turns "we need to think about it" into "when can we go live?"
6 AroFlo + Fergus extraction Medium Completes the ANZ market. AroFlo is strong in commercial/industrial. Fergus is popular in NZ and growing in AU. Together they cover the long tail of mid-market ANZ trades operators.
7 AI outreach generation (LLM email drafts) Low Quick win on top of enrichment. After migration data is imported, Claude generates a personalised outreach email per prospect — referencing their specific platform, trade type, business size, and a calculated ROI projection. SDR sends in one click.

Preventive Maintenance · Compliance Driven

PPM Scheduling — Planned Preventive Maintenance

Body corporate and government facility operators run on PPM schedules — quarterly HVAC services, annual electrical inspections, monthly fire safety checks. FieldInsight's PPM module generates, schedules, and auto-dispatches these jobs from asset-linked maintenance plans.

📅
Asset-Linked Maintenance Plans
Each asset (HVAC unit, switchboard, fire panel) carries a maintenance schedule. When the next service is due, FieldInsight automatically generates the job — no manual intervention.
  • Service frequency: monthly / quarterly / bi-annual / annual / custom
  • Compliance-triggered: AS/NZS 3000 inspection intervals for electrical
  • Asset age and warranty tracking: flag assets approaching warranty expiry
  • Last service date + outcome linked to next schedule
🔁
Recurring Job Templates
Save a job as a PPM template — checklist, parts, estimated duration, required certifications. Every generated PPM job inherits the template and pre-populates the tech's mobile app before they arrive.
  • Template includes: task checklist, required parts, compliance sign-off fields
  • Certification requirements: CCEW, WHS, RCD test record
  • Photo evidence points built into checklist
  • Auto-generate quote from template for customer approval before work

Dispatch Intelligence · Auto + Visual

Allocation Engine — Auto Allocate + Visual PPM Planning

Two modes: full AI auto-allocation for urgent reactive jobs, and guided visual drag-and-drop for planning the monthly PPM schedule. Together they eliminate the dispatcher role — or make your existing dispatcher five times faster.

⚡
Auto Allocate — AI-Driven Assignment
A reactive job comes in. AI evaluates all available technicians across 8+ variables and assigns the best fit in under 3 seconds. No human dispatcher needed for standard reactive work.
  • Variables: skill match · proximity · current load · travel time · customer history · asset familiarity
  • ANZ-tuned: trade licensing verification before assignment (CCEW requirements)
  • Confidence score shown — dispatcher can override with one click
  • Learning loop: outcome data (job duration, customer rating) feeds back into next assignment
  • Estimated savings: replaces $70k+ AUD/year dispatcher role at >20 tech scale
